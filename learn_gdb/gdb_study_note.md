#### 8.5 Examining memory
1. `x/nfu addr` 
	* e.g. `x/80uw 0x55555576e1e0`: u ---> unsigned int, w ---> 4 bytes, 80 amount.
	* e.g. `x/80dc &i`: &i ---> address of i
